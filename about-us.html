About Us

Who Are We?


We Are A Driving School Offering A Secure Shift From L's To P's.


With twenty years of experience in the field, we are Driver Trainers/Instructors and offer a safe transition from Ls to Ps. Driving in today's streets is difficult and requires the right abilities, mindset, and knowledge of the law. Regardless of your age or experience, we acknowledge each individual and their unique needs and learning preferences, and we tailor our instruction to meet those needs.

Our driving instruction is behavior-based, allowing you to progress at your own speed. Regardless of your age, we'll mark off your competencies as you pick up the necessary abilities before moving on to the next phase. Therefore, we will adjust your classes to match your unique skills and schedule, regardless of your pace of learning.

Every lesson will end with a few minutes spent reviewing your progress with us and suggesting areas you could work on if you get the chance to drive with an experienced driver by your side (like your parents or another adult with a valid, full licence).
